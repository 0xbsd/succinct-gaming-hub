    🧠 ZK Concepts

    Commitment Schemes
    Both players commit to their moves using cryptographic hashes before revealing. This prevents cheating or changing moves after seeing the opponent's choice.
    Hiding & Binding
    Commitments are "hiding" (can't see the move) and "binding" (can't change it later). Perfect for fair gameplay!
    Reveal Phase
    After both commit, players reveal their moves and randomness. The commitment is verified to ensure no cheating occurred.

    🔧 How It Works

    1. Choose your move
    move = "rock" | "paper" | "scissors"
    2. Generate commitment
    commitment = hash(move + nonce)
    3. Both players commit
    Share commitments simultaneously
    4. Reveal and verify
    Reveal move + nonce, verify hash
    ✅ No one can cheat or change their move!
